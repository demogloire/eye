About Us

Eye World International

Eye World International, whose name means "world eye international" in English, is a non-governmental organization created in 2012 in the city of Lubumbashi, in the province of Katanga, whose mission is to provide assistance to all children in the world without exception. It intervenes in four key areas: family reunification, schooling of orphaned children, accommodation of children in an orphanage and vocational training and social integration of young people trained in various jobs. It offers the possibility to benefactors to sponsor children they want to help directly. Thanks to this, several children have been taken off the streets and returned to school and others have been integrated into foster families. Eye World International pursues various objectives, including.

  • To provide assistance to orphaned children in difficult situations and separated from their families
  • To ensure the education of children who have been separated from their families
  • Facilitate the social integration of abandoned children
  • Advocate with different partners to mobilize more help for the protection of children

Family reunification

We place children separated from their families in foster families. This provides them with a good environment for their growth.

Empowerment

We strengthen the economic and social empowerment of young people in order to fight against poverty and make them more useful to the community. .

Protection

We fight all forms of violence, exploitation and abuse inflicted on children. This is our daily duty, give to children a bright future.

Education

We pay school fees for orphaned children and those formerly living on the streets. We are convinced that education is the right of every child.

Mision

The organization pursues its mission to help children in difficult circumstances acquire a good education, have better health and decent housing.

Vision

Eye World International's vision is to build a world where the rights of all the world's children are protected. To date, the organization has been active in the province of Tanganyika and plans to expand its activities to all provinces of the Democratic Republic of Congo.

The best way to make a difference in the lives of others is to do it differently.
