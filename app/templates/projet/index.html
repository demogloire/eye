Welcome to World Eye International

We are here to help everyone in need

World Eye International is a humanitarian organization created in 2012 in the city of Lubumbashi where it has its headquarters. The idea of its creation was born after a wave of wars that the Democratic Republic of Congo has known, which has led to massive displacement of populations. Its initiator, the apostle and prophet ESAIE SEBASTIEN KASEBA, had a vision from God to create an NGO whose mission would be to bring help to the people affected by the torments of the repeated wars that the Democratic Republic of Congo has known. With the means at hand and a few donations, the NGO takes care of children in its orphanage and provides schooling to several of them.

Sponsor a child

Many children leave school due to a combination of unfortunate circumstances, exposing their lives to an uncertain future. However, every child in the world, regardless of race or religion, deserves a good education, health care and basic needs (housing, food, drinking water, etc.). The sponsorship from which the children benefit allows them to have access to school and to their vital needs (health, food, drinking water, sanitation). As such, sponsorship benefits not only the child to whom it opens the doors to a better and freer future, but also an entire community.

By sponsoring a child, you create a strong link with him/her, you see him/her grow and become emancipated. The sponsors can, if they wish, come and visit the children they sponsor and enjoy an experience rich in emotions and memories.
