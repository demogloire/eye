Schooling

We take care of the schooling of orphaned children and those separated from their families for various reasons. The beneficiaries are at different levels of study: primary, secondary and university. The assistance also consists of providing the beneficiaries with the school supplies they need and acting as a tutor to the school authorities. We have in place a team of supervisors whose main role is to ensure better academic performance of all children under their responsibility. Supporting the schooling of these children has allowed us to save their future and give them the chance to grow up in a healthy environment. Almost every day, we receive constant requests from children who wish to benefit from our support. In the Democratic Republic of Congo, many children are separated from their families for various reasons such as poverty, death of parents, children accused of witchcraft, etc. This forces them to abandon their studies at a very early age and to be at the mercy of many dangers such as sexual exploitation, forced labor, etc. Thanks to the help we receive from all our partners, we manage to get dozens of children back to school every year.
